How to Play

  • Player X always goes first.
  • Players take turns placing their marks (X or O) on empty squares.
  • The goal is to get three of your marks in a row: horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Use the mode selector to choose between playing against a friend or AI with different difficulties.
  • Click "Restart Game" to start over anytime.
